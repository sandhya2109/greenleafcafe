ABOUT

OUR
CAFE

The concept of our cafe came about from the idea of not
creating fusion beverages but to bring the original taste
of popular "desi" tea to the UK. Founded in 2019, Cafe
Green Leaf has only kept growing while achieving a
strong customer base who regularly get a tea on the go.

Our concept revolves around providing beverages and
snacks which are popular in the Indian Sub continent.
From samosas to Bangladeshi desserts, Cafe Green leaf
continues to attract many different pallets.

HALAL FOOD

Our food is a 100% Halal and we are continuously looking to widen our
menu to serve different options.
